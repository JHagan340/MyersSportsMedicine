Molly Myers

Molly Myers graduated from Virginia Tech in 1995 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Chemistry.

After graduation she began her career in medical research at Johns Hopkins School of Medicine in Baltimore, Maryland studying nuclear envelope formation. Upon moving to Boston, Massachusetts in 1996 she continued her career at the Dana Farber Cancer Institute where she worked in the field of breast cancer research. Leaving academic research in 2000 she joined the Molecular and Cellular Oncology group at Millennium Pharmaceuticals with a focus on prostate cancer research.
